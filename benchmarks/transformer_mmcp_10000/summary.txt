### Summary of Transformer CPU Benchmarks

*   **Top Performer**: 16 Ranks, 1 Intra-op Thread (12.07s). This configuration is 3x faster than using 1 Rank with 16 threads.
*   **Rank Parallelism is King**: For this Transformer model, increasing MPI ranks (processing inputs in parallel) is vastly more effective than increasing intra-op threads.
*   **Critical Warning**: Avoid oversubscribing with `intra=4` or `intra=8`. These settings triggered a performance cliff, making the benchmark up to 42x slower than the optimal setup.
*   **Memory Footprint**: Total memory usage (RSS) peaks at ~27 GB when using 16 ranks. Ensure nodes have at least 32 GB of RAM for this configuration.
*   **Inter-op Insight**: Adjusting inter-op parallelism provided no measurable benefit; keep `inter=1` or `16` as it does not hinder performance.
